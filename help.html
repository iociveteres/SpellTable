Spell Table Features

Click on row with spell to open it's full description. There are links to AoNPRD and d20PFSRD too.

Click on column names to sort them. Click again to change sorting direction.

Input fields can be used for simple search, like "cleric 1" in Access Ways column.
You can make logical expressions with: & (and), | (or), ^ (xor), ! (not) and brackets () â€” f.e. "cleric 1 & (!paladin 1 | !wizard 1)".
Access ways, Casting Time, range and Duration columns support comparison with <, >, = signs, "cleric >2 & cleric <5" or "<2 hours" or ">touch"
You can filter by multiple columns simultaneously.

Check Pin checkbox, so row will not be filtered out and stay on top when sorted.

In the bottom line you will find buttons to toggle display of site name, clearing input fields and unpinning rows.
In the right you will see number of spells satisfying filters. Also there is link for this page and donations.
